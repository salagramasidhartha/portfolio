01 — About

Mapping the intersection of environment, data & policy

I'm an environmental scientist with an M.S. in Environmental Sciences (specializations in GIS & Climate Systems) from the University of Colorado Denver. My work sits at the crossroads of regulatory compliance, spatial analysis, and climate-risk modeling — helping agencies, communities, and organizations make evidence-based decisions.

From conducting 200+ GIS-supported site evaluations for Florida's Department of Health to directing coastal conservation efforts across the Indian coastline and leading disaster-risk assessments for India's apex federal body (NDMA), I bring field-grounded technical depth and the ability to communicate findings to regulators, academics, and community stakeholders alike.

My toolkit spans ArcGIS Pro, Python, Google Earth Engine, HEC-HMS, and sustainability frameworks including the GHG Protocol and double materiality assessment — bridging the gap between raw data and actionable insight.
